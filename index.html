Teacher Adam

I have been a teacher for over 10 years! Most of that time I was living in China. I typically teach English speaking and writing, and some IELTS, but I also love making web pages!

I think building a web page can help students in many ways. They get to learn about logic, technology and design. They learn about how computers “talk” and how to communicate things simply. But most importantly, it is fun!
